The Problem

Why Ethereum Needed
Layer 2 Solutions

Ethereum is the most secure smart contract platform in the world — but at peak demand, it struggles with congestion, high fees, and limited throughput.

Network Congestion

Ethereum handles ~15 TPS. During NFT drops or DeFi events, the network grinds to a halt and transactions queue for hours.

~15 TPS max throughput

Skyrocketing Gas Fees

A simple token swap on Ethereum L1 can cost $50–$200 in gas during congestion, making it inaccessible for everyday users.

$50–$200 avg. swap cost

Scalability Ceiling

For Web3 to reach billions of users, the infrastructure needs millions of TPS — not 15. Ethereum's design prevents this without L2.

1M+ TPS required for scale

The Solution

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is a Layer 2 scaling solution built on Ethereum using Optimistic Rollup technology. It processes thousands of transactions off-chain, then posts compressed proofs to Ethereum — inheriting its full security.
